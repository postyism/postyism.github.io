about

a tattooing journey.

↩︎ return_

yi is a hangzhou-born, toronto-based artist working in tkaronto (known as toronto).

_

having graduated from the university of toronto as an art and new media major student, yi has devoted the majority of their time perfecting their skills in tattooing. they started the tattoo journey in the summer of 2015 as an apprentice, and later a resident artist. as of now, they work with a collective on the west side of toronto.

_

having taken over a decade of drawing lessons due to a lifelong interest that blossomed from a very young age, yi has mastered many tattooing and drawing techniques including line work, hatching and pointillism. in the process of turning traditional drawings into tattoo designs, yi has gradually found a balance between two radically different artistic practices.
